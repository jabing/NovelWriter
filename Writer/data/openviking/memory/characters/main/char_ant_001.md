# Kaelen Thorne

## Age

38

## Appearance

- **build**: lean but powerful
- **distinctive_features**: intricate silver tattoos covering left arm, always wears a fractured crystal pendant, hands permanently stained with arcane energy
- **eyes**: violet (unnatural, glowing faintly)
- **hair**: jet black with silver streaks at temples
- **height**: 6'2"

## Background

Kaelen was once the youngest Archmage in Celestial Spire history, a prodigy who discovered ancient texts describing a 'Perfect World' that existed before the gods fractured reality. During a ritual to glimpse this lost perfection, he accidentally caused a magical cataclysm that destroyed his city and killed everyone he loved. Instead of accepting responsibility, he became convinced the current world was a flawed, broken version that needed to be 'fixed'—by any means necessary. He now leads the Sundered Veil, a cult dedicated to tearing down reality to rebuild it according to his vision.

## Goals

- Shatter the Veil Between Worlds to access primordial creation energy
- Rewrite the fundamental laws of magic and reality
