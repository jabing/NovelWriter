# Aelin Thorne

## Age

25

## Appearance

- **build**: lean and wiry
- **distinctive_features**: faint luminescent tattoos mapping ley lines on her forearms, always wears a silver compass pendant that never points north
- **eyes**: silver-gray, like storm clouds
- **hair**: raven-black, worn in a practical braid
- **height**: 5'8"

## Background

Born to a family of royal mapmakers, Aelin discovered as a child that she could see the invisible currents of magic that flow beneath the world—ley lines, nexuses, and dead zones. Her talent was both gift and curse; the Crown immediately conscripted her into service as an Arcane Cartographer. For years, she mapped magical resources for the empire's expansion, but secretly began documenting something else: the slow, inexplicable weakening of the world's magical veins. Her most formative experience was discovering a 'scar' in reality—a place where magic had been torn away completely—which no official record acknowledged.

## Goals

- Uncover the true cause of the world's fading magic
- Create a complete, unbiased map of the magical world, free from imperial control
