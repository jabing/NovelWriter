# Kaelen Thorne

## Age

28

## Appearance

- **build**: lean and wiry
- **distinctive_features**: intricate silver filigree tattoos on both forearms that glow faintly when he channels magic, always wears mismatched gloves - left hand leather, right hand silk
- **eyes**: amber-gold (a side effect of magical exposure)
- **hair**: dark brown, perpetually tousled
- **height**: 5'9"

## Background

Kaelen was an apprentice scribe in the Grand Archives until he discovered he could 'hear' the resonance of ancient magical artifacts. His natural talent caught the attention of the Mage's Guild, who forcibly enhanced his abilities through experimental arcane tattoos. He escaped their control after realizing they intended to use him as a living weapon, and now operates in the shadows, using his skills to help those the Guild would exploit. The experience left him deeply distrustful of institutional power but fiercely loyal to individuals who earn his trust.

## Goals

- Uncover the truth about the Mage's Guild's hidden experiments
- Find a way to safely remove or control his magical enhancements

## Mannerisms

- Taps his fingers in complex patterns when thinking
- Always examines objects from multiple angles before touching them
- Speaks to inanimate magical objects as if they can understand

## Occupation

Arcane Artificer and Relic Hunter

## Personality

- **desires**: to understand the true nature of magic's source, to create something that helps ordinary people, not just the powerful
- **fears**: his magical enhancements becoming unstable, being used as a weapon by those in power, forgetting who he was before the magic changed him
- **strengths**: exceptional problem-solving with magical artifacts, can read ancient magical scripts, adaptable in dangerous situations
- **traits**: inventive, skeptical, protective, morally flexible
- **weaknesses**: tends to hoard magical knowledge, struggles with authority, overestimates his own abilities

## Role

supporting

## Secrets

- He still carries a small, unstable artifact from the Guild that could reveal their location if activated - but might also kill him in the process

## Voice

Quick, precise speech with occasional pauses when choosing words carefully. Uses technical magical terminology naturally but can simplify concepts when needed. Voice has a slight resonant quality when discussing magic.
