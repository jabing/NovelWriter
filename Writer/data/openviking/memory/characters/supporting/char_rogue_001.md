# Kaelen Swift

## Age

28

## Appearance

- **build**: lean and wiry
- **distinctive_features**: faint silver tattoo of a key on right wrist, always wears mismatched gloves, moves with unnatural silence
- **eyes**: gray, sharp and observant
- **hair**: black, shoulder-length, usually tied back
- **height**: 5'9"

## Background

Kaelen grew up as an orphan on the streets of the capital city, learning to survive through petty theft and information brokering. At 14, he was recruited by the Shadowhand Guild for his natural stealth and sharp mind. He rose quickly through the ranks, but his conscience began to trouble him after a job went wrong and innocent people were hurt. Now he serves as the Guildmaster's assistant, walking a fine line between loyalty to the only family he's known and his growing desire for a legitimate life.

## Goals

- Secure enough wealth to leave the guild and start a legitimate business
- Protect the few genuine friends he has made outside criminal circles

## Personality

- **desires**: financial independence, to prove his worth beyond his criminal past
- **fears**: being trapped or caged, betrayal from those he trusts
- **strengths**: information gathering, reading people, escape artistry
- **traits**: observant, calculating, loyal, sarcastic
- **weaknesses**: cynical worldview, holds grudges, dislikes authority
