# Kaelen Thorne

## Age

28

## Appearance

- **build**: lean, scholarly
- **distinctive_features**: ink-stained fingers, wears spectacles with copper frames, carries a leather satchel filled with scrolls
- **eyes**: hazel, often tired-looking
- **hair**: dark brown, worn tied back with silver thread
- **height**: 5'9"

## Background

Kaelen grew up in the shadow of the Great Library of Aethelgard, where his mother served as a senior scribe. A sickly child, he found solace in stories and histories rather than physical play. When a magical plague destroyed much of the library's oldest wing during his adolescence, he witnessed firsthand how easily knowledge could be lost forever. This trauma drove him to dedicate his life to preservation and recovery, eventually earning his position through sheer determination and an uncanny ability to reconstruct fragmented texts. He carries guilt over several scrolls he failed to save during the disaster.

## Goals

- To complete his life's work: a unified chronicle of the continent's pre-magical history
- To secretly locate and preserve artifacts from a forbidden era the current rulers wish erased
