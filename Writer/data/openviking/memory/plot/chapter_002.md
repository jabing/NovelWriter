# Forbidden Knowledge

## Chapter

2

## Characters

- Elara
- Ignis
- Librarian Maren
- Inquisitor Vorlan

## Emotional Beat

Growing horror as Elara realizes her kingdom is systematically exterminating sentient beings while calling them mindless beasts.

## Key Events

- Elara finds censored references to 'dragon-speakers' in restricted archives
- Her second journey to the peaks, evading academy wards
- First physical encounter with Ignis
- Ignis shows her memories of dragon-human coexistence
- Elara learns about dragon essence harvesting
- She returns to find the Royal Inquisition visiting the academy

## Summary

Elara researches ancient texts about dragon lore, discovering contradictions between official history and fragmented accounts. She secretly returns to the Whispering Peaks and encounters Ignis, an ancient dragon who reveals their species is dying and humans are hunting them.
