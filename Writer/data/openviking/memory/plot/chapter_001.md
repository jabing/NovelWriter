# The Whisper in the Storm

## Chapter

1

## Characters

- Elara
- Mentor Kaelen
- Classmate Finn
- Headmaster Valerius

## Emotional Beat

Wonder mixed with deep unease as Elara encounters something ancient and sentient that shouldn't exist according to her education.

## Key Events

- The Arcane Academy expedition to Storm's Edge
- Elara separates from her group during magical turbulence
- She hears the first dragon thought: 'Help us... fading...'
- Elara sees distant movement in the peaks that defies physics
- She returns to camp changed, unable to explain what she experienced

## Summary

During a dangerous field expedition to study magical storms, novice mage Elara hears a desperate mental plea that isn't human. As her classmates panic about the storm, she follows the voice to discover it originates from the Whispering Peaks, territory forbidden to students.
