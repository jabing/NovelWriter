# The Wounded Earth

## Chapter

4

## Characters

- Elara
- Terra
- Ignis (through mental connection)
- Inquisition scouts

## Emotional Beat

Desperate determination as Elara takes her first active step against the kingdom's plans, fully committing to the dragons' survival.

## Key Events

- Elara journeys to the Crystal Caves using Ignis's directions
- She finds Terra poisoned by magical traps
- First attempt at healing magic through the dragon bond
- Terra shows her visions of collapsing ley lines
- Near-discovery by Inquisition scouts
- Terra reveals only three ancient dragons remain
- Elara learns about the 'Great Weapon' project

## Summary

Following Ignis's guidance, Elara searches for the second ancient dragon, Terra, who protects natural magic ley lines. She finds Terra gravely wounded by Inquisition traps, and must use her growing abilities to heal the dragon while avoiding patrols.
