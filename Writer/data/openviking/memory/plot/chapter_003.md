# The Mentor's Warning

## Chapter

3

## Characters

- Elara
- Mentor Kaelen
- Inquisitor Vorlan
- Captured dragon (later named Ember)

## Emotional Beat

Betrayal turning to reluctant alliance as Elara realizes even those who care for her have kept dangerous secrets.

## Key Events

- Kaelen finds dragon scales in Elara's belongings
- He reveals his grandfather was a dragon-speaker executed for heresy
- Kaelen shows Elara hidden family journals about the bond
- They witness from afar as Inquisition forces capture a young dragon
- Elara feels the captured dragon's agony through her connection
- Kaelen gives her a choice: stop or learn to protect herself

## Summary

Mentor Kaelen confronts Elara about her secret excursions, revealing he knows about her ability. He warns her of the political danger while sharing his own conflicted history with dragon lore. Meanwhile, the Inquisition captures their first dragon.
