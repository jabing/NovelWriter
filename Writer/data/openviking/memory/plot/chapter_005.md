# Exile's Path

## Chapter

5

## Characters

- Elara
- Headmaster Valerius
- Mentor Kaelen
- Inquisitor Vorlan
- Classmate Finn
- Various academy students and faculty

## Emotional Beat

Loss of identity and home, transformed into resolve as Elara fully embraces her role as bridge between species.

## Key Events

- Headmaster Valerius confronts Elara with 'evidence' of her activities
- Public expulsion ceremony before the entire academy
- Kaelen secretly gives her supplies and a map to the third dragon's location
- Elara witnesses the captured dragon being drained of essence
- She feels the magical imbalance as natural magic falters
- Elara chooses to find the third dragon rather than flee the kingdom
- The chapter ends with her looking toward the Northern Wastes where the last dragon sleeps

## Summary

Returning to the academy, Elara finds herself accused of dragon sympathies and expelled. With nowhere to go, she must decide whether to flee entirely or seek the third dragon while the Inquisition closes in on all dragon-related activities.
