## Climax

At the Sunspire Tower, Elara uses her bond with all three dragons to create a counter-ritual as the weapon activates. She must channel their combined magic to disrupt the harvesting process without destroying the city, while the dragons themselves manifest physically to defend her from the Inquisition.

## Logline

A young mage discovers she can communicate with ancient dragons and must prevent their extinction while navigating a kingdom that fears their power.

## Main Conflict

Elara's ability to speak with dragons puts her at odds with a kingdom that seeks to eradicate them for their magical essence, forcing her to choose between her people and the last of the ancient beings.

## Resolution

Elara succeeds in disabling the weapon but at great cost—the dragons' physical forms dissipate, becoming pure spirit beings tied to the land's magic. The kingdom agrees to a truce, establishing protected dragon sanctuaries. Elara becomes the first Dragon Speaker in centuries, bridging the gap between humans and the ancient beings, ensuring neither side forgets the other again.

## Stakes

The extinction of the dragons, the collapse of natural magic, and Elara's own identity and place in the world.

## Theme

The balance between preservation and progress, and the wisdom found in ancient voices.
